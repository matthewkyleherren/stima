Our Heritage

The Enduring
Legacy of Hut 8

We take our name from the legendary building at Bletchley Park where Alan Turing and his team of codebreakers reshaped the course of history. At Hut 8, the spark of genius met the strength of purpose to crack the Enigma code, support Allied victory in World War II, and lay the foundation for modern computing.
